Solutions

Software

POS Systems

We develop POS (point-of-sale) systems, which are systems which facilitate transactions that occur within businesses. Combining cash registers, receipt printers, customer sign-in interfaces, business management interfaces, and customer databases, our system can fulfill most, if not all, small business needs.

Expert Systems

We develop expert systems, which is a type of artificial intelligence which mimics the decision-making abilities of a human using computational and statistical methods. Whether for industrial or corporate settings, our expert systems can help organizations make better decisions.

Air Quality

Activated Oxygen Generator

We develop activated oxygen generators, which are generators of activated oxygen, or ozone (O3). Ozone is capable of removing bacteria, viruses, fungi, and other types of pathogens in various environments. Particularly beneficial for hospital and industrial settings, our activated oxygen generators help ensure that the spread of pathogens in an environment is limited.

Water Quality

Nanobubble Generator

We offer technologies for nanobubble generation. Nanobubbles are exceptionally small bubbles which can be pumped through a system of water for any consumer need. Our state-of-the-art systems will enable organizations to use nanobubbles for various tasks.
